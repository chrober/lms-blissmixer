BlissMixer Similarity Survey

Train your personal distance metric

This survey helps BlissMixer learn your personal sense of music similarity. You will be presented with three songs. Listen to them and select the one that is most different from the other two — the "odd one out".

This is not a quiz — there are no right or wrong answers! The songs are picked randomly and it is purely about capturing your individual taste. If all three songs sound equally similar, just hit Skip and move on.

The more rounds you complete, the better the learned metric will be. 50 rounds is a good start, 200 is optimal.

You can stop at any time and resume later — your votes are saved immediately.
